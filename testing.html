Acne & Guts Health

Ginger & Turmeric

Powerful anti-inflammatories that kill acne-causing bacteria from the inside.

Fatty Fish (Omega-3)

Salmon and mackerel help regulate oil production and soothe red, angry pimples.

Sugar causes insulin spikes, which trigger your glands to produce excess oil, leading to clogged pores.

Some dairy products contain hormones that can disrupt your skin's natural balance and cause cystic acne.

Dullness & Guts Health

Antioxidant Berries

Blueberries and strawberries fight free radicals that make skin look gray and tired.

Vitamin C (Citrus)

Oranges and kiwis help your body produce collagen for that natural "glow" from within.

Both dehydrate the skin, leading to a sunken, dull appearance and emphasizing fine lines.

Too much salt causes water retention and puffiness, making your complexion look lackluster.

Dehydration & Guts Health

Healthy Fats (Avocado)

Monounsaturated fats keep the skin's surface moist, flexible, and strong.

Omega-3 (Walnuts/Seeds)

Helps seal in moisture and prevents the "flaky" texture by repairing the skin barrier.

Poor quality oils can cause inflammation that damages the skin barrier, leading to moisture loss.

Sugar causes "glycation," which weakens collagen and elastin, making dry skin feel even tighter.
