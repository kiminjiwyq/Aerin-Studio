Understanding Your Skin

Understanding your skin type is the foundation of good skincare. When you know your skin, you can:

  • Choose the right products
  • Avoid irritation and breakouts
  • Build a routine that actually works
  • Keep your skin balanced, calm, and healthy


The 4 Main Skin Types

  1. Normal Skin

    Balanced, not too oily or dry. Smooth texture with minimal breakouts.

  2. Oily Skin

    Produces excess sebum. Skin may look shiny, with enlarged pores and frequent breakouts.

  3. Dry Skin

    Feels tight or rough. May have flaking, dullness, or sensitivity.

  4. Combination Skin

    A mix of both—usually oily in the T-zone (forehead, nose, chin) and dry on the cheeks.


How to Identify Your Skin Type

Try this simple method:

  1. Wash your face with a gentle cleanser
  2. Don’t apply any products
  3. Wait 1 hour
  4. Observe:
    • Shiny all over → Oily
    • Tight and flaky → Dry
    • Shine only in T-zone → Combination
    • Comfortable, balanced → Normal

Common Skin Concerns

Your skin type is just the beginning—skin concerns can affect anyone, regardless of type.

  • Acne & breakoutss
  • Redness & sensitivity
  • Excess oil & clogged pores
  • Uneven texture
  • Dark spots & hyperpigmentation


What Might Be Causing Them

Understanding the cause helps you treat your skin more effectively:

  1. Hormones → breakouts, excess oil
  2. Weather changes → dryness, sensitivity
  3. Wrong products → irritation, clogged pores
  4. Dehydration → dull, tight skin
  5. Stress & lack of sleep → acne, uneven tone
  6. Sun exposure → dark spots, premature aging

Simple, Effective Solutions

Keep your routine minimal, gentle, and consistent:

  1. For Acne & Breakouts
    • Use gentle cleansers
    • Look for ingredients like salicylic acid or tea tree
    • Avoid over-cleansing
  2. For Dryness & Dehydration
    • Use hydrating toners & serums
    • Look for hyaluronic acid, glycerin
    • Seal with a moisturizer
  3. For Oily Skin & Pores
    • Use lightweight, non-comedogenic products
    • TTry niacinamide
    • Don’t skip moisturizer
  4. For Sensitivity & Redness
    • Choose calming ingredients (centella, aloe)
    • Avoid harsh exfoliants
    • Keep routine simple
  5. For Dark Spots & Uneven Tone
    • Use sunscreen daily
    • Try vitamin C or niacinamide
    • Be consistent and patient
