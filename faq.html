Skin Health FAQs

Common questions about maintaining healthy skin

Acne can be caused by excess oil production, clogged pores, bacteria, hormones, stress, and sometimes diet.

Dry skin can result from weather, harsh skincare products, nutrient deficiencies, or underlying skin conditions.

Maintain a balanced diet, stay hydrated, manage stress, sleep well, and use gentle skincare products.
